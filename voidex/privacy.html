Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2025  |  Effective immediately  |  Darkbloom Industries™

THE SHORT VERSION: Voidex collects zero personal data. We log nothing about your searches. We store nothing about you. We build no profiles. We sell nothing. If the government demands your data, there is nothing to give — because we never had it.

ZERO LOG POLICY
No search queries stored  ·  No IP addresses logged  ·  No user profiles  ·  No cookies beyond your backend preference

1. Who We Are

Voidex is a global search engine operated by Darkbloom Industries™. We provide search services that index the entire world wide web without geographic censorship, user tracking, or data collection. Contact: DarkbloomIndustries@outlook.com

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not log, store, or retain your search queries
  • We do not collect your IP address
  • We do not use tracking cookies or pixels
  • We do not create user profiles or behavioral records
  • We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party analytics
  • We do not sell, share, or license any data
  • We do not require account creation or registration
  • We do not fingerprint your browser or device

3. What We Do Collect

The only data that touches our systems is the technical minimum required to deliver search results. Your search query is transmitted to our backend proxy, used to fetch results, then immediately discarded — never written to disk or any database. Your backend URL preference is stored locally in your own browser only and never leaves your device.

4. Safe Search Policy

Voidex enforces strict safe search on all search types at all times — web, images, videos, news, and documents. Adult and explicit content is filtered at the API level on every request. This filtering is mandatory and cannot be disabled.

5. Advertising

Voidex displays sponsored ad slots served directly by Darkbloom Industries™. These ads do not involve third-party ad networks, tracking pixels, or behavioral targeting. Advertisers receive no data about users. To advertise: DarkbloomIndustries@outlook.com

6. Affiliate Links

For commercial searches Voidex may display affiliate shopping links to retailers such as Amazon and eBay. If you click and purchase, Voidex may earn a commission. Voidex receives no personal data from these transactions.

7. Search Technology

Results are powered by the Brave Search API. Your query is transmitted to Brave to retrieve results. Brave's privacy policy governs how they handle this. Visit brave.com/privacy/search for details.

8. Government Requests

Because Voidex retains zero user data, we have nothing to provide in response to government subpoenas, court orders, or law enforcement requests. This is enforced at the code level — not just a promise.

9. Security

All communications are encrypted using TLS 1.3. Our backend implements rate limiting (30 req/min/IP), input sanitization, CORS restrictions, and security headers on all responses. Hosted on Cloudflare Pages and Railway.

10. Contact

Privacy questions: DarkbloomIndustries@outlook.com
